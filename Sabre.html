Unit Origins

SABRECOM Evolution

Formerly known as Task Force Sabre, the SSOG has evolved into a powerhouse of unconventional warfare. Now established as a regiment under SABRECOM, it stands as the Philippines' spearhead for global intervention.

Operating alongside the Light Reaction Regiment, SSOG is tasked with the most complex, classified, and dangerous missions directed by PHSOCOM. Due to the sensitive nature of their work, details of their activities are rarely commented on by the SOCOM or Department of National Defense (DND).

"The unit has been deployed to various hot combat zones around the globe to gain experience in modern warfare, ensuring the AFP remains a lethal force on the international stage."
